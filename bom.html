Shopping Strategy

Priority 1 (Do First): Get the non-contact voltage tester and basic screwdrivers if you don't have them. These are safety-critical.

Priority 2: Order the Kasa HS200 switch. Check reviews for the specific model.

Priority 3: Verify existing wire nuts in the switch box are in good condition during inspection. Buy new ones only if needed.

⚠ Before You Buy

Verify neutral wire presence: Remove switch plate (power OFF) and confirm there's a bundle of white wires in the box. The Kasa HS200 requires neutral.

If NO neutral wire is present, you'll need either:
• Lutron Caseta (works without neutral, but requires hub ~$80)
• Run new wire (professional electrician recommended)
